== Description ==

Startquestion is a professional survey software for creating online surveys and surveys of clients and employees. A recognized tool among companies, universities and large organizations.
Get feedback from right users at the right time – exactly when they are interacting with your website.

Main features:
* Multiple surveys on website
* Various different types of questions: NPS single-, multi-choice, text, rating and e-mail.
* Custom logic of survey (conditional questions)
* Advanced settings of display rules (urls, device type, user's behavior)
* Personalize design of your survey
* Connect results with your data and other tools (integrations).

Make better decision based on solid data:
* Who are your users?
* What users think about your website?
* Why users don’t convert?
* Why users don’t sign up?
* How is your offer perceived?
* What features are missing?
* How is your customer service rated?

== Installation ==

1. Upload `website-survey-widget-startquestion.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. [Register account in Startquestion](https://app.startquestion.com/registration?id_partner=1003&redir_code=1&surveyId=519856)
1. [Get widget code from Startquestion account's settings](https://app.startquestion.com/user#widget-installation)
1. Put widget code into settings of plugin.
1. Configure widget in [Startquestion tool](https://app.startquestion.com/user#widget-rules) and analyse results there.
